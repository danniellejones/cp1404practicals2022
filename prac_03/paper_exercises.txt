Exercise 1
Write a menu-driven program to get a valid integer between 1 and 10.
The user would like to select to either times that integer by 20 or subtract 10.


Exercise 2
A Café would like to calculate how many sales they made in a day.
Write a program that continuously gets the sales for the day until the user enters 0.
Then print the total sales for that day.

Extend your Café program. If the total sales for the day is greater than 100 give print good day.
If it is 50 or lower print bad day.
